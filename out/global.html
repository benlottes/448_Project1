mediumShot(none)

mediumShot() takes in no parameters. It assists the AI in playing with medium difficulty. hits is zero until an initial hit is made. It hits randomly until hits is incremented to 1. Once a hit is made the AI shoots up first then right then down then left. It continues on in the direction of a hit until the entire ship is sunk then hits turns to 0 and the AI goes back to shooting randomly
